Geeks Connect Treatment of Kids

November 20, 2015
Date: November 20, 2015 Category: Geeks Connect Tags: Geeks Connect Treatment, Geeks Connect Treatment of Kids

Geeks Connect gives Shriners Children's Hospital monthly charitable donations and has been doing so for over a year and a half now.

Shriner's Hospital for Children specializes in pediatric and medical help for children who have suffered and are suffering serious burns and other life-threatening medical conditions, but who have parents that can not afford the specialized help these babies need.

We believe in protecting and helping little ones

And we know that not all parents are able to afford medical care for their children, and so we are dedicated to making the world a better place by protecting and taking care of what's most precious in this world;...kids.
