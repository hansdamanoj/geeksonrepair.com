Cookies
Geeks On Repair has had ongoing problems with jealous , unscrupulous competitors that wish to hurt our online marketing campaigns by doing what is often known in the online advertising world as "click fraud" , or " fraudulent clicks" , to our pay per click ads.

They do this in the attempts to exaust our daily marketing budget before good customers like yourself can find our online ads.

Therefore , we have a system in place that tracks these unscrupulous people by way of placing "cookies" in their machines. These "cookies" are only placed on visitors who found our website by way of clicking on one of our paid ads, and only after clicking on our paid ads  more then 3 times inside a certain period of time. These "cookies" are solely for our own security ,  and so long as your not clicking on our paid ads more then 3 times in a row , and inside a short period of time , you will not have "cookies" placed , and our system will not effect you in any way. We do not place "cookies" on regular visitors of our website who found us through organic search , by clicking on a hyperlink to our website , or who found us by typing our URL website address into their browser or address bar.

Our website also generates cookies through the company we use for our testimonial audio recordings. This company ( soundcloud) , a very reputable company that allows their customers to upload audio recordings to their website, use their very own "cookies" when one of our website visitors plays one of our testimonial recordings. This is very outside of us, since their cookies serve them for their own purpose but do not serve us in any way, and in no way does Geeks On Repair save or use any data they collect. You can read about the particulars of how they use cookies by going to their websites privacy policy at soundcloud.com
